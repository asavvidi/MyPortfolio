About Me

Full-Stack Developer

My name is Alexandros Savvidis, and I was born in Thessaloniki, Greece. I entered the Department of Computer Science at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in 2018 and graduated in July 2024. During my university years, my interests revolved around web development and mathematics, while I also captured the software technologies and data analysis directions. I am constantly working on improving my technical skills in web development by learning new technologies and best practices.
